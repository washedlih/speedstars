The Exotic Collection

For those who want to drive something truly special.

Bugatti Chiron

A hypercar produced by French luxury car manufacturer Bugatti as the successor to the Veyron. With a top speed of 304 miles per hour, making it one of the fastest and most expensive production cars in the world.

Ferrari LaFerrari

A limited production hybrid supercar built by Italian automotive manufacturer Ferrari. LaFerrari means "The Ferrari" in Italian, in the sense that it is the "definitive" Ferrari.

Mclaren P1

A mid-engine hybrid supercar produced by British automobile manufacturer McLaren Automotive. Only 375 units were produced, making it a highly coveted sports car.

Porsche 918 Spyder

A convertible limited production hybrid sports car manufactured by German automobile manufacturer Porsche. Only 918 units were made, making it a rare collector's item.
