BMW M4 CSL

A high performance version of the M4, which was unveiled in September 2021. The M4 CSL is a limited edition model and only 1,200 units were produced. It has a top speed of 174 miles per hour.

Lexus LFA

A two-seat sports car manufactured by Japanese car manufacturer Lexus, it can accelerate from 0 to 60 miles per hour in just 3.6 seconds and has a top speed of 202 miles per hour. The LFA was a limited production model, and only 500 units were made.

Nissan GT-R

A high performance sports car produced by Japanese car manufacturer Nissan, it can accelerate from 0 to 60 miles per hour in around 2.7 seconds and reach a top speed of over 200 miles per hour.
